Financial Performance Q1-Q3 2020

Published 14th June 2020 by Daniel Porush

The year thus far has been our most profitable yet with net profit exceeding £400 million from Q1-Q3. Our business analysts have forecasted a further profit of somewhere in the region of £100m - £150m more profit to be made this year, which we will continue to reinvest back into Stratton Oakmont to remain one of the top stock-brokers in the world.

Stock prices at all-time high

Published 22nd September 2020 by Jordon Belfort

Stratton Oakmont decided to list its shares in both Shanghai and Hong Kong to raise about £34.5 billion and value the company at £312 billion. The firm will see its shares go public on November 05.The IPO previously received a green light from both the China Securities Regulatory Commission and the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. Alibaba said it will purchase 730 million A-shares through its subsidiary Zhejiang Tmall Technology to uphold its 33% stake.
